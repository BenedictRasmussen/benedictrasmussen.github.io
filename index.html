About me

I've was born and raised in Omaha. Through highschool I performed in music and drama before heading to Madison, SD to attend Dakota State University begin my career in computers. At the time, I was unsure what I wanted to major in and I bounced from Network Administration, to Network Security, to Information Sciences. Eventually, I left Madison and return to Omaha to attend UNO. I spent one year studying vocal music until I couldn't stand the gnawing feeling anymore- I had to learn more about software development. After learning my GenEd credits transferred but not my Comp Sci credits, I started back at square one in Java (having only learned C and Assembly up to that point.)

In May of 2014 I was offered a position as a Software Engineer Intern at (formerly) Solutionary, now NTT Security. After one year, I was offered a full time position with the company, which I happily accepted. At the current time, I work fulltime as a Software Engineer 1 with NTT Security while slowly working towards my Bachelors in Computer Science.

What you might not know about me

As my title says, I also dabble in the outdoors and general fitness. I love to run, and will be participating (note: I did not say competing!) in the Omaha Marathon in the middle of September. I recently purchased a bike and have found that I greatly enjoy trail riding, my favorite track being Tranquility at 120 and Maple. These things aside, I love to rock climb. I have climbed in Arkansas, Minnesota, South Dakota, Colorado, and bits and pieces of the southwest. My greatest achievement of my outdoor activities (and my second greatest achievement of all, losing only to my engagement with an amazing women) was summitting Mount Rainier in Washington in August of 2016. Quite unlike some of the other 14,000 foot peaks in the lower 48 states, it requires an arsenal of mountaineering skills and equipment to safely and successfully summit.
